Breathe promotes stillness and calm with the help of cathartic animations.
Think of these animations as a visual metronome.
Sitting in a comfortable position, breathe in through your nose all the while the animation is expanding,
and then breathe out through your nose as the animation shrinks back down. Repeat this process for as long as you please!
Breathe can be used as the basis for a meditation--perhaps to steady oneself at the start of the day,
or to wind down at the end--or simply as a quick pause during the day, or indeed both! It is entirely up to you!
